Marriage, Couples and Family Counselling Service

Are you in need of specialist Marriage/Couples, Relationship, Family or Individual counselling? Choose Waverley Counselling for a comprehensive therapeutic and comprehensive counselling service. Our team of professional therapists provide a unique and integrated pathway to well-being with an integrated approach that encompasses the development of relationship fulfillment. We empower couples, families and individuals with tools that enable them to communicate and interact with each other and their wider community in a healthy and positive way. A multi-component service our Glen Waverley and Wheelers Hill based counselling specialists facilitate the identification and exploration of issues, supporting clients in the nurturing of existing relationships and the contemplation of new ones.

Caring & Compassionate Counselling

At Waverley Counselling we proudly cultivate a caring and compassionate environment, where you are encouraged to engage in a supportive and collaborative discussion with our highly experienced therapists. This participation clarifies your situation as we partner with you to work towards change by gaining a new and wider perspective.

Non-Judgemental Treatment

Our experienced therapists help you to navigate a myriad of issues from marriage and relationship difficulties, family dispute resolution through to addictions and more, in a non-judgemental manner. Our relationship support services are tailored to the specific needs of couples, families and individuals. It is a discovery process developed to create insight and self-awareness as we work towards resolution.

Zoom and Telephone Counselling

Our Zoom or Phone counselling service provides counselling to clients who are unable to attend our office due to time restraints or working from home. A highly effective method of counselling, we continue to guide and empower many clients in this way. If you are unable to attend our practise, we invite you to enquire about our Zoom or phone counselling service.
